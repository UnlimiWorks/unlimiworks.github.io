About

I'm a programmer hailing from Rabat, Morocco. I discovered programming back in early 2011 while in university, but I found myself more interested in the craft rather than the pure theory material (probably driven by the amount of unrelated subjects spoon-fed as semester modules), leading me to the heavily centered around javascript and web development self-learning path.

I'm an interactive person, a team player, and a person highly driven by achievements and community work. I deeply care about, and look for ways to empower those in need of my skills.

A special shoutout to the FreeCodeCamp community who are one of the main reasons I'm still able to look further in the road and know I can get better
